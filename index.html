Introducing the American Express Centurion Card

For those who spend a lot of money.

The American Express Centurion card is only for people who spend more than $250,000 per year, and you have to be invited by American Express. Sometimes they will send free gifts to users of this or the American Express Platinum card.

Here's some benefits of this card:

  • Hilton Diamond status
  • Delta Platinum Medallion status
  • Personal Centurion concierge

Steps to get approved for a Centurion card:

  1. Get invited by American Express (spend $250,000 per year)
  2. Pay the $5,000 annual fee
